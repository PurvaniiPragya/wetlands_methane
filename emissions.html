Temporal Analysis Project

Wetlands are considered the largest natural source of methane (CH4) emissions in the world. This animation visualizes methane emissions from wetlands in July over a span of 22 years. The dataset contained 22 NetCDF (Network Common Data Form) files for the years 2001 through 2022 with and each file had band layers for 18 model configurations and every month of the respective year. The model 2933 and the month of July was chosen for this project as the methane emissions are at peak for the northern hemisphere post monsoon. To know more about models you can visit the user guide.
